## **\[ BODY \]**

This practice is anchored in a simple, repeatable ritual. There are seven core components to a steeping session. You have already met the first three :: Time, Tea, and a Journal. The remaining four are Intention, Presence, Reflection, and Integration.  
Intention is the question you bring to the cup. It is the flavor of curiosity you wish to explore. Before you begin, you set your intention. It can be a question, a feeling, or a single word.  
Presence is the act of being with the tea. It is the mindful observation of the water, the leaf, the steam, the warmth. It is the gentle returning of your attention, again and again, to the sensory experience of the present moment.  
Reflection is the conversation you have with yourself on the page. Tea gives you time with The Self; and the journaling allows you to see what you have been brewing.  
Integration is how you carry the insights from your steeping session into the fabric of your day. It is the conscious choice to embody the awareness you have cultivated.  
These seven components form the foundation of the practice. They are the seven notes in the scale of creative self-discovery.
