## **4\. The Triakis Protocol Operating Manual**

**The Principle:** The Triakis Protocol is our internal system for quality assurance and project management. It ensures that every piece of work we produce is not just complete, but resonant. It is the geometry of our logic.

**The Process:** At the end of each development phase, before proceeding to the next, we apply the Triakis Gating Protocol. This is a three-part review, conducted by the full project team:

1. **The Technical/Structural View (The Engineering View):** Is it well-built? Is it secure? Is it scalable? Does it adhere to our established design tokens and architectural principles?  
2. **The Experiential/Emotional View (The Media View):** How does it feel? Is it intuitive? Is it beautiful? Does it honor the user's time and attention? Does it speak in the tÅs voice?  
3. **The Meta/Quantum View (The Potentiality View):** Does it open up new possibilities? Does it create new value? Does it move us closer to our ultimate vision? Is it, in a word, *dope*?

**The Rule:** A project cannot proceed to the next phase until it has passed all three gates. No exceptions. This is how we ensure that what we build is not just functional, but true.
