# Creative Steeping: The Three-Tier Ecosystem

**Framework:** Chris Do's DIY / DWY / DFY Model
**Core Insight:** Same problem, different depths of involvement

---

## The Problem We're Solving (At All Three Levels)

**Creative depletion and disconnection from the inner wellspring of expression.**

People need a creative self-care retreat that helps them:
- Reconnect with their creative essence
- Develop a sustainable practice of reflection and expression
- Transform insights into lived experience
- Build community with fellow seekers

The three tiers offer different depths of support for this same journey.

---

## The Three-Tier Model

### Tier 1: DIY - The Digital Sanctuary (10% price point)

**What it is:** Self-paced digital access to the Creative Steeping framework

**What they get:**
- The Creative Steeping Guidebook (digital PDF)
- 7 days of prompts, teachings, and reflections
- Audio recordings of guided steeping sessions
- Digital journal templates
- Access to the Steeping Notes archive (your public reflections)
- Optional: AI journaling companion for personalized insights

**Price:** $77 one-time or $7/month for ongoing access

**Platform:** The digital environment (needs a name - not "Sanctuary" if that doesn't resonate)
- Built with Jazz.tools
- Private digital journal with AI companion
- Community feed to share reflections (optional)
- Weekly content drops (audio, video, prompts)
- Mobile-friendly, offline-capable

**Value proposition:** 
"Steep at your own pace. The complete Creative Steeping framework in your pocket, with an AI companion to deepen your reflections. Perfect for self-directed seekers who want the tools without the hand-holding."

**Scalability:** Infinite. Zero touch from you after setup.

**Customer journey:** 
- They discover the guidebook or hear about Creative Steeping
- They want to try it but aren't ready for live interaction
- They get immediate value and become familiar with your methodology
- 20-30% upgrade to DWY within 3-6 months

---

### Tier 2: DWY - The Steeping Cohort (1x price point)

**What it is:** 7-week guided journey with weekly live sessions and cohort community

**What they get:**
- Everything from Tier 1 (guidebook, digital access, AI companion)
- 7 weekly live Zoom sessions (80 minutes each)
- Cohort community space (Mighty Networks or Jazz-based)
- Direct access to you for questions and guidance
- Accountability and shared experience with fellow steepers
- Certificate of completion / Crown Jewels recognition

**Price:** $777 per person
- Early bird: $677 (first 10 spots)
- Alumni discount: $577 (for repeat participants)
- Scholarship: $333 (2-3 spots per cohort, application-based)

**Cohort size:** 10-15 people (intimate, high-touch)

**Schedule:** Quarterly cohorts (4 per year)

**Value proposition:**
"Steep together. Experience the Creative Steeping journey with live guidance, a supportive cohort, and weekly touchpoints to keep you accountable. This is where transformation happens in community."

**Scalability:** Moderate. You facilitate, but it's a repeatable format.

**Customer journey:**
- They've either done Tier 1 or heard about Creative Steeping from a friend
- They want accountability, community, and direct access to you
- They commit to 7 weeks of showing up
- 80% complete the journey, 40% become advocates/alumni
- 10-15% upgrade to Tier 3 for deeper work

---

### Tier 3: DFY - The Steeping Experience (10x price point)

**What it is:** Bespoke, white-glove Creative Steeping retreat with physical + experiential components

**Two Offerings:**

#### Option A: The Steeping Box + Premium Digital Experience

**What they get:**
- Everything from Tiers 1 & 2 (guidebook, digital access, cohort experience)
- **The Steeping Box** (curated by you and Buckminster):
  - 7 days of premium loose-leaf tea from Deep Creek Tea
  - Custom ceramic steeping vessel
  - Hand-written welcome note from you
  - Printed, bound Creative Steeping journal (premium paper)
  - Artisan tea accessories (infuser, timer, ritual objects)
  - QR codes linking each day's tea to its corresponding prompt/teaching
- Private 1:1 session with you (60 minutes) at the start and end of the journey
- Priority access to all future cohorts and content
- Lifetime membership to the digital environment

**Price:** $1,777

**Value proposition:**
"Steep in luxury. A fully curated, sensory-rich Creative Steeping experience delivered to your door. Every detail handled, from the tea to the teachings. This is the retreat that comes to you."

**Scalability:** Moderate. Requires partnership with Buckminster and fulfillment logistics, but can be systematized.

#### Option B: The Live Retreat Experience

**What they get:**
- Everything from Option A (Steeping Box, digital access, 1:1 sessions)
- **2-3 day in-person retreat** with you, Buckminster, and potentially other practitioners:
  - Intimate group (6-8 people max)
  - Held at a beautiful, intentional space (retreat center, tea house, nature setting)
  - Daily tea ceremonies led by Buckminster
  - Creative Steeping sessions facilitated by you
  - Optional: Guest practitioners (sound healing, movement, art therapy)
  - All meals, accommodations, and materials included
- Post-retreat integration support (3 months of check-ins)

**Price:** $7,777 per person

**Value proposition:**
"Steep in presence. A fully immersive, in-person Creative Steeping retreat where every moment is designed for your transformation. Limited to 6-8 seekers per year. This is the pinnacle experience."

**Scalability:** Low. 2-4 retreats per year max. Highest touch, highest impact, highest revenue per customer.

**Customer journey:**
- They've done Tier 1 or 2 and want the deepest possible experience
- They're ready to invest significantly in their creative renewal
- They become lifelong advocates and often return for future retreats
- They refer high-value clients to you

## The Pricing Psychology

| Tier | Price | What They Think |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| **DIY** | $77 or $7/mo | "I can try this without much risk. If I like it, I'll go deeper." |
| **DWY** | $777 | "This is a real investment, but it's doable. And look at what I get compared to the $77 option!" |
| **DFY** | $1,777 - $7,777 | "This is a luxury, but if I'm serious about this work, it's worth it. And it makes the $777 option look very reasonable." |

**The magic:** When someone sees all three options, they're not asking "Should I do Creative Steeping?" They're asking "Which level of Creative Steeping should I do?"

**The anchor:** The $7,777 retreat makes the $777 cohort feel accessible. The $777 cohort makes the $77 digital access feel like a no-brainer.

**The progression:** Most people start at Tier 1, upgrade to Tier 2 within 6 months, and a small percentage (10-15% of Tier 2) eventually do Tier 3.

## The Naming Question

You're right to question "Sanctuary." It can feel institutional or religious. Here are alternatives for the digital environment:

### Option 1: **The Steeping Space**
- Simple, clear, unpretentious
- "Join The Steeping Space for $7/month"
- Feels open and accessible

### Option 2: **The Steeping Circle**
- Implies community and connection
- "Become part of The Steeping Circle"
- Warm, inclusive

### Option 3: **The Steeping Well**
- Metaphor for a source of nourishment
- "Draw from The Steeping Well"
- Poetic, but still grounded

### Option 4: **The Steeping Commons**
- Suggests shared space and resources
- "Gather in The Steeping Commons"
- Democratic, collaborative

### Option 5: **The Steeping Studio**
- Implies a place of creative work
- "Create in The Steeping Studio"
- Active, generative

### My recommendation: **The Steeping Space**
It's the simplest, clearest, and most flexible. It doesn't over-promise or over-poeticize. It's a space for steeping. That's all it needs to be.

## The Buckminster Partnership

**For Tier 3 (Steeping Box):**

**Proposed structure:**
1. You and Buckminster co-design the box contents
2. Deep Creek Tea provides the tea (wholesale pricing)
3. You source the vessel, journal, and accessories
4. Buckminster handles fulfillment (or you use a fulfillment service)
5. Revenue split: 60% you, 40% Buckminster (or negotiate based on his involvement)

**Why this works:**
- Buckminster gets a new revenue stream and exposure to your audience
- You get his tea expertise and Deep Creek Tea's credibility
- The partnership creates a premium product neither of you could create alone
- It's a natural extension of the tea-as-metaphor that's central to Creative Steeping

**For Tier 3 (Live Retreat):**

**Proposed structure:**
1. You and Buckminster co-facilitate (you lead Creative Steeping, he leads tea ceremonies)
2. Optional: Invite 1-2 other practitioners (sound healer, movement facilitator)
3. Revenue split: 50% you, 30% Buckminster, 20% other practitioners (if applicable)
4. You handle logistics, marketing, and registration
5. He handles tea sourcing and ceremony design

**Why this works:**
- Creates a truly unique, multi-sensory experience
- Leverages both of your expertise and networks
- Positions Creative Steeping as a premium, holistic offering
- Builds a long-term partnership that can evolve
