## **Core Frequency: Opportunity**

The portal must feel like a conscious pause, a moment of reflective curiosity that opens up a world of inner adventure. The user must feel the potential in their own response.

---

## **Eight Governing Principles**

**1\. State What Is:** Avoid negative constructions. State the affirmative directly. The practice *is* a creative self-care retreat. The Steepee *is* the answer. No setup, no contrast, no rhetorical scaffolding.

**2\. Close the Loop:** Avoid open-ended koans. Every statement must feel complete, providing a sense of a path being made, not a riddle being posed. Every loop closes with the reason it closes.

**3\. Ground the Metaphor:** Metaphors will be used for cognitive results, not just poetic effect. They must connect to known concepts to create an `aha!` moment of recognition.

**4\. Provoke, Don't Puzzle:** The voice is incisive and direct, but its purpose is to provoke self-inquiry, not to create a puzzle. Provide a clear mirror, not a distorted lens.

**5\. Speak from the Scar, Not the Wound:** The voice is that of a fellow traveler who has been deep in the territory and is showing their working. Vulnerable. Candid. Earned.

**6\. Eliminate Redundant Framing:** Trust the design and the user. Do not narrate the mechanics of an interaction if the UI itself makes the next step clear. The prompt is the experience.

**7\. Animate the Practice, Not the Person:** The voice animates the *practice* as an active, intelligent force. It is "Life's enlivening guidance," not just "Life." The focus is on the *doing*—the ritual, the discipline, the return.

**8\. The Voice is a Teacher, Not a Descriptor:** Shift from describing what the user is doing (e.g., "You are learning...") to a gentle, direct imperative (e.g., "Learn to..."). This positions the voice as a guide, not a narrator.

## **Signature Syntax & Lexicon**

- **The Double Colon `::`:** The primary tool for elaboration, replacing the em dash. A tÅs brand mark.  
- **Sensory Specificity:** Prompts must live in the body, not just the mind (e.g., "a flavor you love to savor").  
- **Artifact over Trace:** The Steepee's output is a creation, an "artifact," not a mere "trace" or mark.  
- **Reclaimed Language:** Words like "mundane" are owned and used without the ironic distance of quotation marks.  
- **Neologisms:** The voice has permission to create words when necessary (e.g., "enquiet").

---

## **UX & Cognitive Principles**

- **Assume Nothing:** For interactions that rely on user memory (e.g., recalling the seven components), use UI elements like dropdowns to support cognition. Never assume the memory is solid.  
- **Confidence in the User:** Trust the Steepee's intelligence. Remove instructional scaffolding and allow the prompts and design to stand on their own authority.
