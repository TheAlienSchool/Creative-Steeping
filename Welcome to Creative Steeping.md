The Contemplation Blend  
A Custom Herbal Recipe (via https://www.instagram.com/raineandriverapothecary/?ig\_mid=681586C0-1A18-4726-8C9D-A58769CC12F6\&utm\_source=igweb\&launch\_app\_store=true)

Damiana: Heart-opening, sensual clarity  
Bacopa: Memory \+ insight enhancer, known to stimulate cognitive pathways  
Gingko: Vascular expansion for flow — in mind and blood  
Peppermint: Mental refresh, digestive support, and energetic reset  
Calendula: Emotional immunity \+ gentle solar clarity  
Eyebright: Visionary support, clarity of focus (both internal and external)

This blend is designed to support your perceptual intimacy with the essence of yourself. Each herb offers a signature: some open the heart, others clear the lens, some stabilize the signal. When steeped together, they activate both contemplation and communication within the self.

Essences are the unseen threads of being that weave the fabric of our existence. They are not the tangible objects we hold, nor the roles we play, but the invincible presence that defines our unique existence. When we steep into our essence, we align with the purest expression of who we are, free of pretense, fully alive, and deeply present.

The word “essence” originates from the Latin essentia, meaning “being” or “substance.” It is derived from esse, which means “to be.” This etymological root reveals that essence is not about what we have or what we do, but about the very act of being. It is the fundamental nature that cannot be reduced or stripped away.

Before Each Session

• Choose your tea ritual space  
• Brew your blend (begin steeping 5-10 mins before session)

• Take 3 deep, conscious breaths

During Each Session

• We open with a brief check-in \+ energetic framing

After Each Session

• Observe what is still steeping in you  
• Reflect, rest, or share with your co-Steeper(s) if inspired
