# THE STEEPING SPACE: CINEMATIC SCORE INSTRUCTIONS

*An Architectural Guide for Sonic Generation.*

This document provides explicit instructions, prompts, and compositional parameters for generating the environmental audio of The Steeping Space. These parameters can be handed directly to an agentic audio partner (e.g., Suno, Udio, ElevenLabs, or a human composer) to guarantee the output is viscerally medicinal and aligned with Affirmative Architecture.

---

## 1. The Compositional Philosophy (The Physics)

The audio of the Steeping Space is not meant to "entertain." It is meant to "entrain."
*   **The Baseline:** 528Hz (The frequency of repair and stillness). All harmonic roots must anchor here.
*   **The Pace:** 60 BPM or lower (to match and lower the resting heart rate).
*   **The Vagus Resonance:** Utilize slow attack times and extreme release times. No abrupt percussion. Sounds should emerge from the silence like ink expanding in water.

## 4. Agentic Prompts for Transmission (`/transmission`)

For generating or mixing human spoken word (Historical Scores, Sage Voice):

*   **Voice Texture:** The voice must be recorded or generated with a close-proximity microphone effect (proximity effect adds warmth and low-end bass to the human voice).
*   **Pacing:** The delivery must be 30% slower than conversational speech. Pauses between sentences should last a full 2 to 3 seconds. The silence is where the practitioner processes the insight.
*   **Processing:** Apply a gentle low-pass filter to remove harsh "S" and "T" sounds (sibilance). The voice must never pierce the ear; it must wrap around it. Add a very subtle, long-tail reverb (3-second decay) mixed at 10% volume so the voice feels like it exists inside the Steeping Space.

## 5. The Golden Rule of Evaluation

If the generated audio makes the heart rate accelerate, discard it.
If the generated audio demands active attention, discard it.
The score must be felt before it is heard.
